About TopPH Colleges

TopPH Colleges is an informational website designed to highlight some of the most prestigious universities in the Philippines.

Purpose

The goal of this website is to provide students with a quick overview of leading colleges in the Philippines and help them explore their academic opportunities.

Explore Universities

Users can browse universities, read brief descriptions, rate colleges, and access the official websites of each institution.

Educational Project

This website was created as a web development project to demonstrate modern UI design and interactive web features.
